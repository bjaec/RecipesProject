Egg Recipe

Description

Just making normal cooked eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put oil on the frying pan and let it heat up.
  2. Crack the egg over the pan once the pan is heated up.
  3. Spray salt and pepper over the egg.
  4. Flip the egg once one side is ready & repeat.
  5. Enjoy!
